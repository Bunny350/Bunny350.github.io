About Oitswilliam Pang's Minecraft content

Oitswilliam Pang, formerly Bunny 350 built some creations in Minecraft to everyone from 2015 to 2019, then switched to resource pack industry in 2019, with the first creation with NI00, and now open-source Mojave mine craft.

He is also a live-streamer of his survival world, who shows the creativity with limited resources.

He was formerly operated his own game server called Bunny 350 Server, with wanted games, but was defunct due to development issues.

Currently, he builds tunnels that are long, and can be accessed without going to the nether.
