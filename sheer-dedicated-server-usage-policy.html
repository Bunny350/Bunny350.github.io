Sheer Dedicated Server Usage Policy

Updated on April 9, 2024.

This shows the policies made for Oitswilliam Pang Sheer Dedicated Server made for Minecraft. You may agree to this policy to visit Sheer Dedicated Server.

Admins in Sheer Dedicated Server, including Oitswilliam Pang may reserve rights to temporarily enable or disable building modes, open or close viewer to builder upgrade form and change game modes for viewers to either adventure or spectator mode.

If you feel unfortunate for getting banned due to usage policy violations, or do not believe you have violated such policy, you can request here for an appeal, which also known as unbanning. Please note that you can only use this appeal once during your first ban only, and your appeal may be denied anyways if you have been banned more than once, caught using some type of unfair advantages, behaving offensively, if any, or building materials that are not safe for younger ages (see Building section).

We do not accept unbanning via money or getting unbanned by buying. It is an illogical ransom.

IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER: SHEER DEDICATED SERVER IS NOT AN OFFICIAL MINECRAFT SERVER. IT IS NOT APPROVED BY OR ASSOCIATED WITH MOJANG OR MICROSOFT.

Definitions

  1. Minecraft comes with two editions, Java Edition, which is explicitly shown and Bedrock Edition, which won't show what edition. We call them the "game". Sheer Dedicated Server is made for both versions of the game.
  2. First-time requirements are sets of requirements that you have to do when doing things just for the first time.
  3. "Viewer" is a term in which users in Sheer Dedicated Server can only view around, but prohibited interactions.
  4. "Builders" is a term in which users in such group can build and interact. See the Building section below for the list of policies apply.
  5. "Follow" in this policy is a term in which the user is showing them following Oitswilliam Pang in other platforms, or subscribed in YouTube.
  6. Unfollow-then-re-follow is a move that users will first unfollow and then re-follow, for like an example trying to get their ban evaded. Please note that this do not work in SDS and some other servers.
  7. "Mods" in this policy means community modifications, which adds or changes functionality of the thing that normally won't be seen.
  8. "Vanilla" in this policy means stock / normal version that mostly only be modified by the provider.
  9. "Building" as a section is a term that means someone can build.
  10. "Punishments" is a set of rules for both admins and users that prevent them from using if they violate policies.
  11. IP Ban may ban innocent players in the same public IP address some of the rule breakers are at. Some may use ranged bans.

Sections apply to all users including not yet following

Sheer Dedicated Server streams are allowed to be distributed to watchers who are not following Oitswilliam Pang in either platforms. However there are limitations that people must respect; You, whether as Viewers or builders, are not allowed to reverse-engineer, build or distribute the entire world of Sheer Dedicated Server, or parts of it, including buildings or features, to people who are not following Oitswilliam Pang.

First-time requirements

You should agree that you must be a follower or subscriber of Oitswilliam Pang and has filled the form from Sheer Dedicated Server main page before joining it in Minecraft.

You must agree that unsubscribing or unfollow Oitswilliam Pang will unlist you, prevent you from joining Sheer Dedicated Server in the future.

If you re-subscribe or re-follow Oitswilliam Pang after unsubscribing in less than 48 hours once, you are not required to manually re-fill the form.

You may not use another game account linking the same / similar account to join or re-join Sheer Dedicated Server, you may not evade bans for punishment reasons by using the method called unfollow-then-re-follow.

Client requirements

For Java Edition of the game, Use vanilla, whitelisted modded clients, such as Optifine, Badlion (pronounced bad-lion) or Forge with whitelisted mods. These are the only whitelisted clients. Unlisted clients are prohibited and some of their features should be avoided.

For Bedrock Edition of the game, vanilla is the only version we are permitted.

Mods

In Java Edition of the game, the whitelisted mods are Replay Mod, Optifine, shaders mods, and Armor HUD. If your mod was not listing here, you may need to disable it.

The game in Bedrock Edition mods that gain unfair advantages are prohibited and should be avoided, such players are advised to use vanilla versions of it.

Chatting

You are not allowed to force admins for, or trick them to get higher-end privileges including operator rights.

You must not act badly such as swearing, excessive use of negative words or use of naughty words.

You may not spam, advertise or promote unrelated materials, including but not limited to other servers for the game.

Building

This section applies to builders. Builders are the higher-tier option which is available via the same request form. You will receive Builder rights if the request succeed.

You must agree that you should not be disruptive to other users. You may not vandalize other people's builds, including but not limited to destructing them or dumping things unnecessarily to others.

You are allowed to create your own rules, but for safety reasons, SDS Usage Policy will always be enforced in top priority. Therefore, you may not create sections of rules that allow users to break SDS Usage Policy. Staff members, including admins, may ignore your rules when they are doing administrative works. You may not enforce your rules to others, especially strangers in-game.

You may not spawn excessive amount of mobs, although we tolerate up to 100 mobs per person.

You should agree that you are not allowed to build inappropriate materials that are either graphic, unsafe or not suitable for younger ranges.

When lower-tier users are in adventure mode, you may avoid giving them items.

Leaving Sheer Dedicated Server

Users who want to leave Sheer Dedicated Server from their existence (or opt-out from SDS) is allowed. But please note that you must be joined SDS for at least 90 days to opt-out. When you want to opt-out, you need to be kept following Oitswilliam Pang in either platform. You can opt-out if you are banned, but it will not remove your ban. You cannot opt-out if you have unfollowed Oitswilliam Pang.

You can request us to remove your structures via one-click request, but it is required to remove your inventory from SDS. When the request is successful, your username will be hidden to others on structures you have built to other players. You are allowed to join back at any time after 3 days, and your username will show back to others if the structures weren't removed.

Punishments

Sheer Dedicated Server has punishment systems. One of it is made for unfollowers, which prevents them from accessing if they have found unfollowed Oitswilliam Pang in any platforms linked, and structures built by them will have [STALKER] at the beginning of their names. Besides this, users who offend in-server may receive various punishments. You may refer to this list if you are unsure;

  1. Kick: This will occur if the user caught by admins broke a section of the rules for the first time.
  2. 10-day ban: This will occur if the user fails to follow the rules, They can be banned with the same duration up to 3 times if they caught breaking after 15 days of ban expire, if possible. They can appeal to remove ban (known as requesting an unban) if they are banned for the first time.
  3. Permanent ban: This ban occurs if the user never follow the rules, caught failing to follow the rules again within 15 days after the ban expired or caught failed following the rules after successful appeal (also known as unban request) within 21 days.
  4. IP Ban can occur if the admins of Sheer Dedicated Server caught someone abusing accounts to violate policies.
