Finding OIWP products manuals

To find the manuals for OIWP products and the open source project, you have to go to GitHub and then find the matching product name's repository. You can find the older versions by seeing the commit history or visiting it's past branches.

For BabyDrone series drone frames and AirTag UTC, the first version was still in Google Drive, so if you want to keep it's former glory during repair, you have to head to Google Drive link of the repository.

Can't build them even after following the manual?

You can contact us or the community for the exact installation or report for the design errors.

You can either join our Discord server or Facebook group for the community support, or contact via Facebook or X for the official support (weekends 3AM-4PM UTC).
